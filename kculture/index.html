      Culture facts

      Hallyu means Korean Wave

      The term describes the global spread of Korean pop culture, including music, dramas, film, beauty, and food.

      Food is a social experience

      Many Korean meals are shared at the table, with side dishes and conversation being part of the experience.

      Tradition and modernity mix

      Modern Korean culture often blends fast-moving trends with long-standing customs, design, and seasonal rituals.
